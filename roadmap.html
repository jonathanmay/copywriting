So, what's next?

We've learned a lot from putting this together. There are a few bugs. There are even more features we'd like to add. We recognise that good fundraising copy is also subjective, and very little copy survives first contact with a donor.

With this in mind, we've decided both the code, and the the roadmap for development, would be better if they were both "open". In terms of the code, this means you can see, use, and modify it under the MIT licence, and if you'd like to help build it, drop us a line.

With the roadmap, it means we want you to tell us what's missing. Our public roadmap is linked to here on Trello, or click below. Please make suggestions and feed back what you'd like to see next directly on that roadmap.
